No appearance

Abstract

No Appearance is an interactive object, the theme of which is the religion and science and technology in modern society.

In No Appearance, the figure of Buddha set in the modern building. The audience can interact with the buddha via the sound. The phrase of Buddha changes according to the sound in real time.
