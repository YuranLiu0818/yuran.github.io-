SUPA

Abstract

Every planet generated from the vortex has its own life cycle. When their lives end, they become tons of particles floating in the universe. Where will they travel after this?

The universe gives birth to planets. The life happens on the planet. So-called “Intelligent creatures” began to simulate the cosmic system model digitally. In the simulative system, universe also gives birth to planets…Here seems to have a loop in a loop. Many of the assumptions about the universe seem small and shallow compared with the vastness of the unknown in the universe. However, computer systems are created by humans. It can show and observe our imaginary universe system as humans’ wishes. So we created our SUPA demonstrating our specific aesthetic and imagination about the universe.
